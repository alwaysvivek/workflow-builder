Understand exactly what each action does.

Action Description
Clean Corrects grammar, spelling, and removes unnecessary whitespace. Keeps meaning intact.
Summarize Condenses the text to capture main ideas and crucial details.
Keypoints Extracts the most important points and presents them as a bulleted list.
Simplify Rewrites the text in plain language, making it easy for anyone to understand.
Analogy Provides a creative real-world analogy to explain the concept.
Classify Categorizes the text into a specific topic, intent, or domain (e.g., "Sports", "Complaint").
Tone Analyzes the sentiment (positive, negative, etc.) and explains why.
